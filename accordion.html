Accordion

Design

Accordion blocks are a versatile solution for organizing and presenting large amounts of information in a structured and digestible format, allowing users to expand and collapse sections of content as needed.

What should I do before planting trees and shrubs?

Before planting, ensure your trees and shrubs are well watered. If you can't plant them immediately, place them in afternoon shade during the summer. Avoid planting during the heat of the day. Make sure you have the right location for your plant to thrive, considering soil and exposure requirements. Most plants don't do well in poor draining soil, so consider conducting a drainage test if you're unsure.

HTML Structure

Below is the HTML code snippet illustrating the structure of our Accordion block:
