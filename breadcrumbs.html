Breadcrumbs

Design

Our design principles for breadcrumbs emphasize simplicity, clarity, and consistency. We advocate for clear and concise breadcrumb labels, intuitive navigation paths, and consistent styling to ensure that users can easily understand and interact with the breadcrumb navigation system.
