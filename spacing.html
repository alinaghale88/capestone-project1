.l-mb-1 1 rem Bottom

Padding

The Padding section of our design system provides classes to easily add padding to elements. These classes are designed to be intuitive and flexible, allowing you to specify padding values for different sides or combinations of sides.

Here are the available classes and their corresponding padding values:

Class Name Value Unit Scope
.l-pl-1 1 rem Left
.l-pr-1 1 rem Right
.l-pr-1-5 1.5 rem Right
.l-px-40-dt 40 px Horizontal
.l-px-1 1 rem Horizontal
.l-pt-10px 10 px Top
.l-pt-1 1 rem Top
.l-py-10px 10 px Vertical
.l-py-0-dt 0 - Vertical
.l-py-1 1 rem Vertical
.l-p-10px-12px 10, 12 px Vertical, Horizontal
